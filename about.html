Who We Are

About CyberAT

We started CyberAT because small businesses deserve the same quality of IT support and security that enterprise companies get — at a price that actually makes sense.

Our Story

Built for Businesses That Can't Afford to Fail

CyberAT was founded with a single mission: bridge the gap between enterprise-level IT security and small business affordability. For too long, small and medium businesses have been left behind — patching together consumer-grade solutions and hoping for the best.

We saw businesses lose thousands of dollars to ransomware attacks, suffer days of downtime from preventable failures, and struggle with technology that slowed them down instead of moving them forward. That had to change.

Today, CyberAT provides the same caliber of IT management and cybersecurity that Fortune 500 companies rely on — delivered with the personal touch and responsiveness that only a dedicated team can offer. No call centers, no runarounds. Just real people who care about your business.
